WITHOUT REGRET

"Without Regret" is a 3D narrative adventure and puzzel game. The game is currently ongoing with a release date of May 2026.

The artstyle is a 3D low poly models with slightly stylized textures.

In "Without Regret", my main role is the environmental artist. Additionally, my other roles are a prop artist and a 2D artist.

I created the nature assets in the game, such as the trees, ground textures, bushes, leaves, and the skyboxes.

I use Photoshop to draw the textures of the sky and plants, while using alphas to create the leaf textures. I also use trim sheets to generate the textures.
