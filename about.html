Get to know Hope

For as long as I could remember, I always loved drawing. But there was a certain time in elementary school that created the artist that I am today.

Around second and third grade of elementary school, I decided to search the internet for drawing tips and tutorials. With the help of of these step by step processes at a young age, I tought myself how to make realistic drawings on my own throughout the years.

In middle school, high school, and my beginning years of college, I learned more art tips that helped improve my skills.
